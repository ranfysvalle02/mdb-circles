How to Install

iPhone & iPad

Open with the Safari browser.

  1. 1Tap the Share button .
  2. 2Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
  3. 3Confirm by tapping "Add".

Android

Open with the Chrome browser.

  1. 1Tap the three-dot menu (⋮).
  2. 2Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen".
  3. 3Follow the prompts to confirm.

Desktop Computer

Use Chrome or Edge.

  1. 1Look for the Install icon in the address bar.
  2. 2Click it and then click "Install".
  3. 3The app will now be in your computer's app list.
